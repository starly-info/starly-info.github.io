--- title: Home ---

Starly help you read and memorize manuals with zero effort. There are no ads, no third-party tracking scripts, no login required. You are ready to get full content only with your email.

... And it is completely free!

Starly let you subscribe to newsletters. Each newsletter help you learn and practice a manual. Once subscribed, each user receive daily chunks of the manual (theory) and some questions or exercises (practice).

Motivation: We all love manuals and we all know the truth behind the acronym RTFM. Hovewer, too often, we are too lazy to read an entire manual back to cover. We can overcome our lazyness one chunk at a time. Reading theory in the bathroom, in a queue, before sleeping and each time we have time to read an email. Questions help us to memorize the knowledge better and ispect a little deeper something that we could have left behind.

We must remmeber what ancients have always said:

A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step.
Excerpt from the text Tao Te Ching.

Nota: If you are not a lazy person or if you already read the manual, not despair! You can join as a deliberate practice exercise. Try to answer the questions and reading the theory one more time, help you consolidate your memories. How much do you remeber?

For techie friends, email are sended with mailchimp.
